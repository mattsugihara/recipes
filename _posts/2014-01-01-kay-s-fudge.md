---

## Ingredients

- 2 squares chocolate
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 Tbsp. vanilla
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate and butter on low heat
2. Stir in milk and bring to a boil
3. Add sugar, vanilla, and salt
4. Cook to a soft ball
5. Pour onto a marble slab
6. Cool, beat, and eat
