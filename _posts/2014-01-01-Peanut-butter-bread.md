## Ingredients

- 2 cups all purpose flour
- &frac14; sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- &frac12; teaspoon salt
- 1-1/3 cup milk
- &frac12; cup peanut butter (plus a bit extra)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 325&deg;f
2. Combine dry ingredients
3. Mix in milk
4. Mix in peanut butter
