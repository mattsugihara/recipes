## Ingredients

 - 4 pound lamb leg
 - 3 cups zesty italian dressing
 - &frac34; cup red wine vinegar
 - 1 white onion, coarsely diced

## Directions

1. Trim the leg of lamb and cut to 1&frac12; pieces
2. Combine everything and let marinate for 4-24 hours
3. Skewer and grill
