##Ingredients

- 1 &frac12; cups AP flour
- 3 &frac12; teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 &frac14; cup milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons melted butter

##Directions

1. Combine dry ingredients.
2. Melt butter. Add milk, add the egg, then mix.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
4. Griddle it.
